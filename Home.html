Who are we

We are Young&Capable and we believe in empowering young aspiring South African entrepreneurs. Our mission is to counter the Nation's high unemployment rate by providing a space where people can connect, share opportunities and learn from established entrepreneurs and their peers. By doing so, we can help foster job creation.

How it Works

We have three important features important to your entrepreneurial journey. The first consists of live seminars hosted by accomplished entrepreneurs in efforts to learn and connect as you gain new insights. The second is a Discussion Forum that facilitates an exchange of ideas and fosters networking amongst people with shared interests. Lastly, we offer an Opportunity Board. This is a forum where people can post and discover opportunities such as job listings, funding opportunities or internships.
