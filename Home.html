About

Young&Capable is a community-driven platform that is dedicated to empowering young and aspiring entrepreneurs in South Africa. Our main goal is to counter the high unemployment rate in the country by creating a space where people can connect, share opportunities, and learn from established entrepreneurs and their peers. We believe that by providing a supportive environment for entrepreneurs, we can help to foster job creation and drive economic growth in South Africa. Our platform offers a wide range of resources and tools that are designed to help young entrepreneurs succeed, including access to mentorship, networking opportunities, and educational resources. At Young&Capable, we are committed to creating a community that is inclusive and welcoming to everyone, regardless of their background or experience level. We believe that everyone has the potential to be a successful entrepreneur, and we are here to provide the support and resources needed to help turn those dreams into reality.

How it Works

Live Seminars

Attend live seminars hosted by accomplished entrepreneurs in efforts to gain new insights and network. You will also have access to or even share learning resources such as short courses and resume guides.

Peer-to-Peer support

Enjoy connecting with your fellow peers on various shared interests as you build your network and share knowledge on the discussion forum platform.

Opportunity Board

Do you need to apply your knowledge in the real world or search for funding? This feature allows you to find opportunities catered to your interests.
