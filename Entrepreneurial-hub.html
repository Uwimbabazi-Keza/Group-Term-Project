Entrepreneurship Hub for Africans

This is a platform which connect budding entrepreneurs to established ones through seminars and provide them with tools for success. The goals of the platform will be to connect, empower, and educate aspiring entrepreneurs. Participants will have an opportunity to raise questions and participate in peer to peer mentoring. This will hopefully lead to a better understanding of entrepreneurship and result in new business ventures and jobs for the community.
